Compliance and Command:
A Truthmaker Approach to Imperative and Deontic Logic

Kit Fine (NYU)
Fri. 3rd June, 6pm; Sat. 4th June, 5pm
Room B33, Main Building, Malet Street
Birkbeck College, University of London

The 3rd biennial Edgington Lectures will be delivered by Kit Fine on Friday 3rd and Saturday 4th June, 2016, at the Department of Philosophy, Birkbeck College, London. Professor Fine will speak on Compliance and Command: A Truthmaker Approach to Imperative and Deontic Logic.

The lectures are free to attend and all are welcome.

Graduate Workshop

In tandem with the Edgington Lectures, Professor Fine will lead a graduate workshop, which graduate students in philosophy and related disciplines are invited to attend. This will run from 10am to 3pm, on both days of the lectures, the 3rd and 4th June.

The workshop is also free, but we ask those wishing to come to write to edgingtonlectures@gmail.com to register.
